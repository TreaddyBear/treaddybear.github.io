Star Meter — design mockup

A throwaway prototype to feel the meter. The remaining stars wait on the right; the next one slides in to meet your fill, locks with a sparkle, and banks to the left. Internal points are hidden — this is purely the player-facing feel.

How to read it: stars sit at even marks (1/N … N/N), so the layout looks clean and linear, but the fill eases — the first star comes quickly, each next one slower (your "first fastest, last slowest"). A mistake knocks the fill back; earned stars are permanent (rank only ever goes up), so a mistake just delays the next star. Tell me what feels off — pace, where the first star lands, how the star "reaches" you, the sparkle, the knockback amount.

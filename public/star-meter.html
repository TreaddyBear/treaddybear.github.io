Star Meter — design mockup v4

Stars wait on the right. Each one only breaks off during the last half-increment (1/6 of the bar for 3★, 1/10 for 5★) and slides in to meet the fill at its milestone — except the final star, which stays put while the fill climbs all the way to it. On completion the whole bar lights gold.

Model: score = 5·(par − elapsed) + 100·grass% − Σ mistakes (mistakes 1000, then ×0.9 each). Stars at the thresholds shown in "this star". Meter fills from one threshold to the next, resets on each star. Mistakes/time only ever push the fill back — a won star is permanent.
